Bug & Feature Rewards

The app gets better when you tell us what's broken or missing. To say thank you, every month we pick the most significant bug reports and feature requests and credit those users with a free month of My Care Companion.

How it works

🐛 Top 5 bug reports

Each calendar month we pick up to five bug reports as the most significant. Each pick earns the reporter one free month.

💡 Top 5 feature requests

Each month we pick up to five feature requests that shipped to production. Each pick earns the requester one free month.

What "most significant" means

When the team reviews each month's submissions, we weigh four things:

  • Impact on user safety or care — anything affecting medication tracking, alerts, or PHI gets the most weight.
  • How many users were affected — bugs we'd been hearing about from multiple families rank higher than one-off issues.
  • Originality — feature ideas we hadn't already planned ourselves rank higher than ones already on our roadmap.
  • Quality of the writeup — clear steps to reproduce, screenshots or screen recordings, and a description of what you expected vs what happened make a report easier to act on.

How to submit

  1. From any screen, open the Support page.
  2. Click + New Ticket.
  3. Pick Bug Report or Feature Request from the type dropdown.
  4. Describe what happened. Adding screenshots or a short screen recording (desktop only) makes the report dramatically more useful.
  5. Submit. That's it — you're in the running for that month.

What if I don't get picked?

If we receive a real bug report or thoughtful feature request and it doesn't make the top 5 that month, you'll still hear back from us — usually a quick canned message thanking you for the time. Your submission isn't ignored; we just couldn't credit everyone, and we wanted you to know it landed.

Fine print

  • The free month is delivered as a single-use promo code locked to your account, valid for 90 days.
  • Only the canonical reporter of a unique bug is eligible (if your report was marked a duplicate of someone else's, the credit goes to the original reporter — but you'll automatically be notified when the issue is fixed).
  • For feature requests, all users who requested the same feature are eligible when it ships.
  • Account must be in good standing.
  • We reserve the right to skip a category if a month doesn't produce any qualifying submissions.
Thank you for being part of this. Feedback like yours is the reason My Care Companion gets to be a thoughtful, careful product instead of a generic one.
