Medical emergencies

MyCareCompanion is a tracking and journaling tool, not a medical device. It does not diagnose, treat, cure, or prevent any condition. If you or your child is having a medical emergency, call 911 (or your local emergency number) and contact your healthcare provider — do not rely on this app.

Common questions

How do I get an account?

You can create an account from the home page. Accounts are free to start, and the optional subscription unlocks the full feature set for $15/month, cancellable any time.

Where is my data stored?

All health information is stored on encrypted servers in the United States. Your data is never sold or shared with advertisers. AI-assisted pattern analysis runs under a signed Business Associate Agreement with AWS; names and free-text notes are stripped before any AI call by default. See the Privacy Policy for full detail.

How do I delete my account?

Sign in, open Settings → Danger Zone → Delete My Account, and follow the email confirmation flow. You have a 30-day grace period to restore your account if you change your mind. After 30 days the data is permanently deleted. Full details are in our Terms of Service.

How do I report a problem with another family member or a chat message?

Inside the app, tap the flag icon next to any chat message you want to report — that opens a pre-filled email to our support team. Family owners can also remove members directly from Settings → Family Members. We review reports within 24 hours and can suspend or remove accounts for abusive behavior. See Terms § 3.4 for the full policy.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Sign in, open Settings → Subscription, and tap Manage Subscription — that opens the Stripe billing portal where you can cancel any time. Cancellation takes effect at the end of your current billing period.

I forgot my password.

Use the Forgot password? link on the sign-in page. We’ll email you a reset link.

I’m a journalist, researcher, or healthcare provider with questions.

Please email support@mycarecompanion.net with a short description of your inquiry and we’ll route it to the right person.
