Frequently Asked Questions

Is my data secure?

Yes. CareCompanion uses enterprise-grade encryption, secure authentication with optional two-factor authentication, and follows healthcare data privacy best practices. Your family's data is never sold or shared.

How long does it take to set up?

Most families are tracking within 5 minutes. Create an account, add your child's profile, and start logging. The AI begins learning your child's patterns immediately.

Can multiple caregivers use the same account?

Absolutely. Invite parents, grandparents, nannies, teachers, and even therapists to collaborate on care. Each person gets their own login with appropriate permissions.

What if my child doesn't have an official diagnosis?

CareCompanion is helpful for any child with complex care needs. Many families use it while seeking diagnosis or for related conditions like ADHD, sensory processing differences, or developmental delays.

How does the AI pattern detection work?

Our AI analyzes your logged data to find correlations between factors (like sleep quality and next-day behavior). It combines global medical knowledge, patterns from similar children, and your child's unique history. The more you log, the smarter it gets.

Can I share data with my child's doctor?

Yes. Export detailed reports for appointments, or grant viewing access to providers directly in the app. Many doctors appreciate receiving structured data rather than trying to reconstruct events from memory.

What if I need to cancel?

Cancel anytime from your account settings with no questions asked. Your data remains available for export for 30 days after cancellation.

Is there a mobile app?

CareCompanion is a responsive web app that works beautifully on any device - phone, tablet, or computer. Simply visit the website from any browser. No app download required.
